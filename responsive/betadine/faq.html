Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about Betadine® consumer products.

What are Betadine® products?
Betadine® products are antimicrobials that significantly reduce the bacteria on the skin.
Read more.
What is the active ingredient in Betadine® products?
Povidone-iodine is the active ingredient in Betadine® products. Read more.
How long have Betadine® products been around?
Betadine® products have been used in homes and hospitals worldwide for nearly 50 years to reduce bacteria that can potentially cause infection. Read more.
In what formulations is Betadine® available?
Betadine® products are available in the following formulations: Betadine® Solution and Betadine® Skin Cleanser for use at home. Read more.
Where can I buy Betadine® products?
Betadine® products are available at online retailers or stores near you. Find out more about purchasing Betadine® products.
What is proper handwashing with Betadine®and why is it important?
Proper handwashing, including with Betadine® Skin Cleanser, can help kill germs and significantly reduce bacteria on the skin.
Learn the steps of proper handwashing.
Should Betadine® products be part of my first aid kit?
Antiseptic solutions and skin cleansers are first aid kit essentials. Build your own first aid kit.
Do Betadine® products stain skin or clothing?
Betadine® products contain the active ingredient povidone-iodine, which may turn the skin a yellowish brown and stain clothing. Read more.
