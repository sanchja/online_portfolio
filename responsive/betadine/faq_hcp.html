Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about Betadine® with these frequently asked questions.

What is the active ingredient in Betadine® products?
Betadine® products contain povidone-iodine to help to reduce bacteria that can potentially cause skin infection. Read more about how Betadine® products work.
What is the clinical effectiveness of Betadine®?
Povidone-iodine, the active ingredient in Betadine® products, are active against a broad spectrum of pathogens in vitro.1 Learn more about the antiseptic properties of Betadine® products.
What formulations of Betadine® products are available?
Betadine® products are available in numerous formulations and package sizes to accommodate the varied needs of physicians and nurses. Learn more about Betadine® professional products.
How are Betadine® products best used in surgical hand scrubbing and healthcare personnel hand washing?
In accordance with CDC guidelines, proper handwashing with Betadine® products is an important step in the prevention of nosocomial infections in the healthcare setting. See the handwashing recommendations.
What is the history of Betadine® products?
Betadine® products have a long history of antisepsis. It has been trusted by healthcare professionals worldwide for nearly 50 years as an important first line of defense against topical infections. Today, Betadine® products are among the leading antiseptics in U.S. hospitals. Discover more about Betadine® products.
Where can I buy Betadine® products?
Purchase Betadine® professional products via your medical supply dealer or our Customer Service Center at 800-877-5666.

Betadine® products are for external use only. Single use will reduce the risk of infection from extrinsic contamination with products not labeled single use only. Do not use in the eyes or if you are allergic to povidone-iodine or any other ingredients in this preparation.

Stop use and ask a doctor if irritation, sensitization, or allergic reaction occurs. When using these products, prolonged exposure to wet solution may cause irritation or, rarely, severe skin reactions. In preoperative prepping, avoid ‘pooling’ beneath the patient.

Keep out of reach of children. Please read full product label before use.
