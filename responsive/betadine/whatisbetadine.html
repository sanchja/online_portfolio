What is Betadine®?

Betadine® antimicrobials for home use

An antiseptic that promptly kills germs

  • Betadine® products have been used by homes and hospitals worldwide for nearly 50 years to reduce bacteria that can potentially cause skin infections
  • Povidone-iodine, the active ingredient in Betadine® products, is active against a broad spectrum of pathogens in vitro 1
